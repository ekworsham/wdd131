BLOWER MAINTENANCE

Introduction

Please review the following essential components of the blower and schedule a time with your manager to demonstrate the proper care for this equipment.

Purpose:

By maintaining this equipment, we recognize the following benefits:


⚫Durability and Longevity: Regular maintenance helps protect and prolong the lifespan of equipment, reducing the frequency of repairs and part replacements.
⚫Cost Savings: Maintenance saves costs in the long run and because of the resulting longevity and prolonged lifespan, it reduces the cost of equipment replacement.
⚫Reliability: Well-maintained equipment is more reliable, reducing the risk of breakdowns and downtime during critical operations or projects.
⚫Efficiency: Properly maintained equipment operates more efficiently, which can improve productivity and performance.
⚫Safety: Equipment that is well-maintained is safer to operate, reducing the risk of accidents or malfunctions that could harm operators or others nearby.
⚫Quality of Work: Maintained equipment ensures consistent quality of work output, meeting or exceeding expectations for clients or stakeholders.

Starting Proceedure

Following these steps, starting (and turning off) blowers can be accomplished with ease:
⚫Turn on/off switch to the “on” position.
⚫Turn choke on, if blower engine is cold.
⚫Give the pull cord a short pull.
⚫Let engine warm up for 30 sec. before revving engine to full throttle.

Safety

Required Protective Equipment:
⚫Fluorescent safety vest are mandatory for anyone operating a backpack blower on any property at any time
⚫Long hair MUST be pulled up and away from the intake fan at all times.
⚫Eye protection
⚫Ear plugs

Conclusion

Mastering the maintenance of a blower through comprehensive training ensures not only efficient and effective of equipment but also promotes safety, enhances productivity, and upholds our commitment to maintaining high standards for our equipment and for our goal of service excellence.
